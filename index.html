K.F.Penmoor

K.F.Penmoor is a surrealist visual artist working in the shadowy realms of dream and distortion. Influenced by the haunted visions of Beksinski and the shifting sands of subconscious imagery, Penmoor crafts unsettling landscapes where form decays and emotion lingers.
